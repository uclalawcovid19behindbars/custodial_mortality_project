Inmate Death Notification – Manuel Corona

February 18, 2022

Buckeye (February 12, 2022) – Inmate Manuel Corona, 30, ADCRR #244835, died on February 12, 2022.

Corona was admitted to ADCRR custody in 2013 after he was sentenced out of Cochise County for aggravated assault.

His assigned housing location was ASPC-Lewis.

All inmate deaths are investigated in consultation with the county medical examiner’s office.
